Fraud Detection — Select a fraud detection method to verify that restaurant charges on your credit statement also follow the method:
• Add — Adjusts cents in the grand total by adding the digits in the dollars (for example, $123.00 to $123.06).
• Mirror — Adjusts cents in the grand total by mirroring the dollars (for example, $123.00 to $123.21).
• Repeat — Adjusts cents in the grand total by repeating the dollars (for example, $123.00 to $123.12).
